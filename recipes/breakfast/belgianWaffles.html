Belgian Waffles

Yields: 4

Ingredients

2 eggs
2 cups all purpose flour
1 ⅓ cups milk
½ cup vegetable oil
1 tbsp white sugar
4 tsp baking powder
¼ tsp salt
½ tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

1. Preheat waffle iron.

2. Beat eggs in a large bowl with hand beater until fluffy.

3. Beat in flour, milk, vegetable oil, sugar, baking soda, salt and vanilla just until smooth.

4. Spray preheated waffle iron with non-stick cooking spray. Pour mix into hot waffle iron. Cook until golden brown. Serve hot.
