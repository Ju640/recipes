Omelette cups

Yields: 16

Ingredients

36 frozen tater tots, thawed
8 eggs
3 tbsp milk
Salt and pepper to taste
Your fav omelette fillings

Instructions

1. Push 3 tater tots in each cup of a non-stick muffin tin. (If tater tots still frozen, microwave a bit)

2.Bake at 425o F for 10 minutes. Remove from the oven.

3.Place your favorite omelette fillings in each muffin tin on top of the tater tots.

4.In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt and pepper. Pour egg mixture into each muffin tin.

5.Bake at 350 F for 20 minutes or until fully cooked. Good with hot sauce.
