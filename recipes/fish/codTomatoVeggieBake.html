Cod Tomato Veggie Bake

Yields: 4

Ingredients

1 can Diced Tomatoes with spice
1 lb white Fish
Broccoli and Cellery
1 onion cut in slices
Italien Spices, Parsley, Salt and Pepper
Veggies to taste

Instructions

1. Put the fish in an oven safe dish.

2. Add the rest of the ingredients on top of the fish.

3. Set the oven to 375F. Cook for 20 minutes, the juice of the tomatoes will be boiling.
If the veggies are cooked, it is ready. Internal temp of fish should be 145F.
