Cajun Salmon

Yields: 2

Ingredients

1 lemons, sliced into rounds
salmon fillet (1 lb)
Kosher salt
Freshly ground black pepper
2 tbsp. melted butter
1 1/2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tbsp. whole-grain mustard
1 tsp. Cajun seasoning
1/2 tsp. fresh thyme leaves
Pinch of crushed red pepper flakes

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350Â° and line a large baking sheet with foil.

2.Lay lemon rounds in an even layer in center of pan. Place salmon on top and season with salt and pepper.

3. In a small bowl, combine melted butter, garlic, mustard, Cajun seasoning, thyme, and red pepper flakes. Brush all over salmon.

4. Bake until salmon is cooked through, about 25 minutes. Switch oven to broil, and broil for 2 minutes, or until butter mixture has thickened.
