Cheesesteaks Stuffed Peppers

Yields: 4

Ingredients

4 bell peppers, halved
1 tbsp. vegetable oil
1 large onion, sliced
16 oz. cremini mushrooms, sliced
Kosher salt
Freshly ground black pepper
1 1/2 lb. sirloin steak, thinly sliced
2 tsp. Italian seasoning
16 slices provolone

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 325ยบ.
Place peppers in a large baking dish and bake until tender, 30 minutes.

2. Meanwhile, in a large skillet over medium-high heat, heat oil.
Add onions and mushrooms and season with salt and pepper.
Cook until soft, 6 minutes. Add steak and season with more salt and pepper.
Cook, stirring occasionally, 3 minutes. Stir in Italian seasoning.

3. Add provolone to bottom of baked peppers and top with steak mixture.
Top with another piece of provolone and broil until golden, 3 minutes.
