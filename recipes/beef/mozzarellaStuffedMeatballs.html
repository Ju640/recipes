Mozzarella Stuffed Meatballs

Yields: Many - Will have to count

Ingredients

mozzarella cheese
1 lb ground beef
1 lb hot italian sausage
½ tsp garlic powder
2 tsp salt
1tsp black pepper
1 cup bread crumbs
¼ cup parmesan cheese
2 eggs
½ cup whole milk
½ cup chopped parsley
2 jars spaghetti sauce

Instructions

1. Cut mozzarella into smallcubes.

2. Mix the rest of the ingredients together.

3. Roll balls and put the cheese in the center.

4. Put in slow cooker with tomato sauce Cook on high for 2 hrs
or
Place balls on grid pan and put in oven for around 30 minutes at 400F.
