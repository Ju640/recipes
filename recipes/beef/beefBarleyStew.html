Beef Barley Stew

Yields: 6

Ingredients

1 lb beef stew meat, diced small
1-1/4 cups diced carrot, fresh or frozen
1 onion, diced or pureed
3 ribs celery, diced
15 oz can tomato sauce
1/2 cup pearl barley
5 cups water
1 tsp parsley flakes
1/4 tsp pepper
1 tsp granulated sugar
2 tbsp Beef Bouillon powder

Instructions

1. Add all ingredients to a gallon sized freezer bag.

IN SLOW COOKER : Cook on high for 4-5 hours or low 8-9 hours.
