Eggroll - Mom's Recipe

Yields: 6

Ingredients

2 lbs ground beef
6 Celery sticks
2 big onions
1/2 Cabbage
3 eggroll dough boxes
2 eggs (eggwhites)
Soy sauce

Instructions

1. Cook the ground beef with the vegetables, except the cabbage. Drain. Faire frire jusqu’à ce soit dorer. Étendre sur les essuie-tout pour enlever le gras.

2. Add cabbage to boiling water with 2 tbsp of sugar. Boiled for 10 minutes. Drain.

3. Mix cabbage with ground beef and vegetables. Add salt and pepper, and soy sauce to taste.

4. Can add onions salt, garlic salt or celery salt to taste.

5. Spread out eggroll dough on counter. Paint on eggwhites on the edges.

6. Add a spoonfull of beef mix in the middle.

7. Close the dough with fingers.

8. Fry until golden.

9.Spread on counter on scott-towels to absorb
