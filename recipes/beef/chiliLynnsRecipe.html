Chili - Lynn's Recipe

Yields: 6

Ingredients

2 tbsp oil
1 cup chopped onion
¼ cup chopped green pepper
1 lb ground beef
3 branch celery
1 can red kidney beans
1 large can of Italian spiced tomatoes
1 - 2 tbsp chili powder
1 ½ tsp salt
1 tbsp vinegar
¼ tsp pepper
Sugar to taste
1 - 2 can tomato sauce
1 - 2 can tomato paste

Instructions

1. In pan, heat up oil. Cook onion, green pepper, and beef.

2. Heat all ingredients on stove top for 1 hour stirring frequently, making sure stove is not too hot (medium heat)

3. Make sure it doesn’t burn on.

4.Put everything in pot and keep on low once it boils

5.Makes enough for 4-6 people
