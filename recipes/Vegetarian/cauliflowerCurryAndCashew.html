Cauliflower Curry And Cashew

Yields: 4

Ingredients

1 large cauliflower, leaves, and stems trimmed
1 red bell pepper, seeded and thinly sliced
3-4 red potatoes (cut in bite size pieces)
1/2 onion, chopped
2 garlic cloves, crushed
Toasted cashews and chopped cilantro, for garnish
2 cups vegetable broth
2 cups sweetened coconut milk
2 tablespoons yellow curry powder
1 teaspoon cumin
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (or to taste)

Instructions

1.Add the whole cauliflower, red peppers, potatoes, garlic and onions to the slow cooker.

2.In a medium bowl whisk together vegetable broth, curry powder, cumin, and cayenne pepper.
Pour mixture into the slow cooker and stir well to coat cauliflower and veggies.
Cover and cook on high for 2-3 hours or on low 4-5 hours.
The cauliflower will likely be done before this but will continue to get more tender as it sits.

3. About 10-15 minutes before serving, stir in coconut milk. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper.
Serve the slow cooker cauliflower warm, cut into wedges into individual bowls, and top with the curry sauce, toasted cashews, and freshly chopped cilantro.
