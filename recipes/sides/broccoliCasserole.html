Broccoli Casserole

Yields: 1 big pan

Ingredients

2 heads broccoli (or 12 cups frozen broccoli florets)
2 can of cream of chicken soup
4 tbsp mayonnaise
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 cup of dried bread crumbs
1/2 cup of grated parmesan cheese
2 tbsp of melted butter

Instructions

1. Cut broccoli in small pieces and place in a casserole dish.

2. Combine soup, mayonnaise, and cheese and pour over broccoli.

3. Mix crumbs, Parmesan, and melted butter and sprinkle over broccoli and bake uncovered for 40 mins in 350 degrees F oven. Makes 6 servings.
