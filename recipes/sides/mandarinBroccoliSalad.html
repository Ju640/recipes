Mandarin Broccoli Salad

Yields: 2 big sides

Ingredients

3/4 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup sugar
4 tsp cider vinegar
4 cups fresh broccoli florets
1 small red onion, halved and sliced
1/2 cup raisins
1/2 cup pecan halves, toasted
1 can (11 ounces) mandarin oranges, drained

Instructions

1. Whisk mayonnaise, sugar and vinegar; set aside.

2.In a salad bowl, combine broccoli, onion, raisins and pecans. Drizzle with dressing; toss to coat. Gently stir in oranges.

3. Refrigerate, covered, at least 3 hours before serving.
