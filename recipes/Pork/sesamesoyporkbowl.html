Sesame Soy Pork Bowl

Yields: 4

Ingredients

3 cups Jasmine Rice
2 lbs Ground Pork
1/2 cup Mayonnaise
1.25 tbsp Sriracha
2 cups Schredded Carrots
3/4 cup Sweet Soy Glaze
3/4 cup Sesame Dressing
Crispy Fried Onions
Cilantro
1 Lime
2 tsp Sugar
4 tbsp Butter

Instructions

1.Make rice

2.Cook the ground pork.
Stir in carrots, sesame dressing, sweet soy glaze, sugar.
Cook until carrots are softened, pork is cooked through, and sauce has thickened.
Season with salt and pepper.

3.Mix mayo and sriracha for sauce.

4. Plate rice, then pork, then mayo sauce, crispy onions, cilantro, and lime wedge.
