Zucchini Boats

Yields: MANY

Ingredients

4 large zucchinis
2 tsp olive oil
4 links sausage links, thinly sliced
1/2 cup italian breadcrumbs
3/4 cup shredded mozzarella
1/2 cup shredded fontina
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 cup chopped fresh basil

Instructions

1.Preheat oven to 350 F.

2.Score zucchini (like you're dicing an avocado) and scoop out insides into a large bowl.

3.In a large skillet over medium heat, heat oil. Add saussage and cook until seared, 6 minutes.

4.Transfer to a large bowl and add breadcrumbs, 1/2 cup mozzarella, fontina, garlic, and basil.

5.Spoon mixture into zucchini and top with remaining 1/4 cup mozzarella.

6.Bake until zucchini is tender and cheese golden, 15 minutes.
