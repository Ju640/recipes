Andre Ham Pork Balls

Yields: MANY

Ingredients

1 lb ground ham
1 lb ground pork
2 cups breadcrumbs
2 cups of milk
2 eggs
1 tsp salt
Sauce:
1 ½ cups brown sugar
1 ½ cups vinegar
¾ cups water
1 tbsp dry mustard

Instructions

1.Make small balls.

2.Cook in sauce. 40 minutes in 350 F oven.
