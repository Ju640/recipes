Carbonara Pasta

Yields: 4

Ingredients

pasta
Bacon
Egg
Parmesan/reggiano
Pepper
Garlic

Instructions

1. Boil pasta

2. Cook bacon “chopped” keep bacon oil

3.Put a garlic clove in bacon

4.1 tsp black pepper with bacon

5.Mix egg and 20g cheese

6.Put pasta in bacon

7.Turn the heat down and mix in the mix of egg and cheese.
