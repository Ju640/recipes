Alfredo Sauce

Yields: 4

Ingredients

¼ cup butter
1 cup heavy cream
1 clove garlic, crushed
1 ½ cups freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

1. Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium low heat. Add garlic.

2. Add cream and simmer for 5 minutes.

3. Add cheese and whisk quickly, heating through.
