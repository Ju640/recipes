Basil Halloumi Chicken

Yields: 2

Ingredients

2 chicken breasts
1 8oz block of halloumi cheese
2 cartons red cherry tomato
4 large basil leaves
2 tsp salt
2 tsp pepper
⅓ cup olive oil
Basil oil:
⅓ cup olive oil
3 garlic cloves
2 large handfuls of basil
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
Juice from ½ a lemon

Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F

2. Turn the block of halloumi on its side and cut it into three to four even slices. Set aside.

3. Pour the olive oil, salt, pepper, into a large plastic bag. Put the tomatoes and chicken in and massage until everything is evenly coated in the seasoned oil. Dump everything into a foil lined baking sheet.

4. Top the chicken with the large basil leaves and then the slices of halloumi. Bake for 15 mins at 350 degrees F. Then turn on the broil setting and cook for another 10 minutes or until the cheese is golden brown and the chicken is cooked.

5. Take the chicken out and let it continue to cook the tomatoes for 10 mins.

6. Add the olive oil, garlic, salt, pepper, and lemon juice to a blender and blitz for 30 secs. Next add the basil until the consistency of pesto.
