Chicken Parm

Yields: 4

Ingredients

4 chicken breast
2 eggs - beaten
3/4 cup bread crumbs
28 oz can tomato sauce
3 tbsp parmesan cheese
1/2 cup mozzarella cheese
1/4 cup parmesan cheese, grated

Instructions

1.Mix breadcrumbs and 3 tbsp parmesan.

2.Dip chicken in eggs, then in bread crumb mix.

3.Fry in oil until golden brown.
Put in baking dish.

4.Spread tomato sauce over mix.

5.Sprinkle with mozzarella and parmesan.

6. Bake at 350 F for 1 hour, or until chicken is cooked.
