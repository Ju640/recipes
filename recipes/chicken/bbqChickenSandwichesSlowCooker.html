Bbq Chicken Sandwiches - Slow Cooker

Yields: 6

Ingredients

2 1/2 lbs chicken breasts or tenders
1 1/2 cups BBQ sauce, we use Kinders
1/2 sweet onion sliced
1 tablespoon smoked paprika
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon kosher salt
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1 teaspoon dried oregano
6 buns or sandwich rolls
3 tablespoon butter softened
Slaw: 3 cups shredded cabbage
1 cup chopped cilantro
1 chipotle pepper diced, from the can with adobo sauce
1/2 cup mayo
1 tablespoon lime juice
salt/pepper to taste

Instructions

1. In a small bowl mix paprika, garlic powder salt, brown sugar, oregano and mix until combined

2.Add sliced onions to slow cooker and place the chicken on top. Sprinkle the seasonings on the chicken. Pour bbq sauce over the chicken.

3.Cover and cook on high 3-4 hours or low 5-6 hours.

4.In a small bowl add the mayo, chipotle, and lime juice whisk until combined and set aside.

5.In a medium bowl add the cabbage, and cilantro add the dressing to the slaw and mix until combined. Add salt and pepper to taste.

6.Remove chicken to a cutting board and shred with two forks. Place chicken back into slow cooker and stir to coat with the sauce.

7.Spread the softened butter over the buns. Heat a skillet or griddle to medium high heat, add the buns butter side down into the skillet. Cook 2-3 minutes or until golden brown.
