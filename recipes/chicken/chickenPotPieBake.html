Chicken Pot Pie Bake

Yields: 4

Ingredients

1 lb Chicken Breast
12 oz Frozen Peas and Carrots thawed
2 cup Frozen Diced Potatoes thawed (like Hash Browns or Potatoes OBrien)
1 Onion diced
2 can Cream of Chicken Condensed Soup
1 cup Water
1 can Refrigerated Crescent Rolls.
1/2 tsp Poultry Seasoning
Salt and Pepper to taste.

Instructions

1.Grill chicken breasts until cooked through. Allow to cool.

2.Grill diced onions until transparent. Set aside. Dice chicken into 1/2" cubes.

3.Preheat oven to 350. Grease a 11x13" baking dish.

4. In a large skillet, combine chicken cubes, diced onion, water, potatoes and peas and carrots. Stir and cook over medium heat until ingredients are warm (5-10 minutes).

5. Add soup and poultry seasoning and continue heating for another 5 minutes. If sauce looks too thick, slowly add a little more water until desired consistency is reached.

6. Pour chicken mixture over baked crust and spread evenly. Unroll other can of crescent rolls and pinch perforations to seal into a sheet. Place over top of chicken mixture and press into sides of dish.

7. Bake for 25 minutes or until top is golden brown.
